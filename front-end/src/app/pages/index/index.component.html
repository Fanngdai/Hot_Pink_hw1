How To Play

  • Enter a word for the bot to guess.
  • Start guessing the word the bot chooses for you.
  • On the bottom left side of the screen there is a row of letters that can be toggled to help eliminate and include letters.
  • When either you or the bot guesses the word, a winning message will be displayed.
