About Jotto

Jotto is a two player word game in which each player attempts to guess that other player's word. Each player has to come up with a five-letter word without repeating characters. The players take turns guessing five-letter words. After a guess, the other player then says how many letters in the guess match a unique letter in their word. This repeats until one of the players guesses their opponent's word.
